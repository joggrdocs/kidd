Add `@kidd-cli/core/test` export with test utilities for handler, middleware, and integration testing. Includes `createTestContext`, `runHandler`, `runMiddleware`, `runCommand`, `mockPrompts`, `setupTestLifecycle`, `createWritableCapture`, `stripAnsi`, and `normalizeError`. Also extends `createContext` to accept optional `prompts` and `spinner` overrides for dependency injection.
